About the Liberal Party

Platform:

We believe that all Americans should have the right to work with dignity, an adequate income, fair housing, collective bargaining, medical care and education. We believe live free of fear and burdensome monopolies. We believe the statesman, businessman, and common man all have it in their interest to better society by ensuring these rights. We believe in preserving and strengthening the institutions that have made our country great. Access our full interim platform here.
